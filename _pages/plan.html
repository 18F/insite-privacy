Privacy Threshold Analysis

Overview

Per NIST 800-122, Privacy threshold analyses (PTAs) are used to determine if a system contains personally identifiable information (PII), whether a privacy impact assessment (PIA) is required, whether a system of records notice (SORN) is required, and if any other privacy requirements apply to the information system.

GSA PTAs are comprised of a simple questionnaire, which should be completed by the system owner in collaboration with the data owner. PTAs are useful in initiating the communication and collaboration for each system between the privacy officer, the information security officer, and the information officer.
